About this website / FAQ

What is mypowerisout.org?

Mypowerisout.org is a website for people to report a power outage and get real-time information and advice. It has been optimized for mobile devices. We are also building Apple and Android apps to make the service even easier to use.

How can utility companies use this website?

Utility companies can use the dashboard to create location-specific alerts that are immediately viewable by all users in that location (e.g. Update for Cambridge, MA: Crews are in your area; we expect to have power restored in 2 hours). Utility companies also have real-time access to the data, including a map of reported outages and outage statistics. We are actively building other tools/capabilities and we welcome your feedback/suggestions.

How can utility companies benefit from using this website?

As you know, communicating with customers during power outages is challenging and expensive. By using this website, utility companies can reduce call volume (cost) and improve customer satisfaction.

How much does it cost?

Nothing. We built this as a service to the community.
